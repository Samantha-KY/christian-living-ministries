INTRODUCTION

The church in Rwanda has been long time serving
as an organization rather than being an organ.
Because of this, the church has many church
attenders rather than transformed people and
Gospel living. Based on the great commission in
Matthew 28:18-20, should be doing church
planting to solve the problem of sin through a
community of believers who proclaim the Good
News about Jesus Christ. With this situation in
mind, Christian Living Ministries comes into the
field to make a contribution in solving that
problem.

OUR MISSION AND VISION

MISSION

To evangelize and disciple people for holistic transformation of lives into the mind set of Christ.

VISION

To see and have, believers who are Gospel centered, Christ-centered, Spiritled and Biblical based and Great Commission oriented.

OTHER INFORMATION RELATED TO SMALL GROUPS

In terms of empowering Christian families; we
train women to be the pivot of transformation in

been equiped how to sahe Gospel with their
families.
